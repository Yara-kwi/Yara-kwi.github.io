About CosMos

CosMos is a community of space enthusiasts, scientists, educators, and curious minds united by a passion for the cosmos.

Founded in 2023, our club aims to foster a deeper understanding of astronomy, astrophysics, and space exploration through educational programs, events, and collaborative projects.
